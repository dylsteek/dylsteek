Welcome to Georgians Tennis Club

 

We are a fun and friendly tennis club that provides a beautiful venue to learn and play tennis. Situated in the heart of Crouch End Playing Fields, our facility offers tennis services to all, from beginners to world-class players.

We have twelve tennis professionals & four strength conditioning trainers with extensive coaching and playing experience and provide our members with the opportunity to play on a variety of court surfaces.

Our Club

Tennis

We have 8 high-quality, multi-surface tennis courts , playable all-year round. Our Adult & Tennis in the Park programmes are open to all ages & abilities and run throughout the week.

Academy

Georgians Tennis Academy provides elite level training to up & coming tennis stars. We have a world-class coaching team to help young players maximise their potential.

Memberships

We have 7 different Club Memberships on offer with varying levels of club access including: Group & Individual Coaching, Gym access, Academy & Family options.

Shop

Check out out extensive range of Head tennis equipment; from rackets & clothing to bags & balls.

Fitness

We have an on-site Gym with Olympic standard equipment & its open 7 days a week. Our personal trainers are also on-hand to help get you into shape!

Clubhouse

Our clubhouse features a Pro Shop & a cafe serving hot food & drinks. The clubhouse is available to hire for day & evening events.
